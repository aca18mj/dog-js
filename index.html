Use guide:

Buttons:

There are 4 buttons that control Dog's behaviour

The "Stand Up", "Sit Down", "Sleep" buttons change position of the Dog accordingly

The "Reset" button changes Dog's position to default

Mouse:

Depending where user click relatively to Dog's position different effect may be applied

When click above the dog - if the Dog is standing then it will reach up, if the Dog is lying on the ground then he will get up

When click beneath the Dog - if the Dog is reaching then it will stand down, if the Dog is already standing then it will lie down.

When click on the front leg when standing - Dog will raise it's paw to shake it
